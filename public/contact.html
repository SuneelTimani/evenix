FAQ

How do I book an event?

Open Book Event, select the event and ticket type, then continue through booking or payment.

Why can't I access the admin dashboard?

Only accounts with the admin role can open admin routes and organizer tools.

How do I update my details?

Use the avatar menu in the navbar and open your profile page to save name, password, WhatsApp, and notification settings.
